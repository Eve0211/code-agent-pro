# Self-Reflection Phase

> Phase 11: Learn and Improve

## Purpose

Summarize lessons learned, continuous improvement.

## Input

- Entire development process

## Output

- Reflection document

---

## Reflection Questions

### 1. What Went Well

- What went well?
- What methods were effective?
- What experiences can be reused?

---

### 2. What Could Be Improved

- What difficulties were encountered?
- What could be faster?
- What could be avoided?

---

### 3. What We Learned

- New technologies?
- New methods?
- New insights?

---

### 4. What to Do Next Time

- What needs to change?
- What needs to add?
- What needs to remove?

---

## Notes

1. **Be honest** - Don't hide issues
2. **Be specific** - Not generic
3. **Be actionable** - Improvements must be executable
